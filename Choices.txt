

median to group for between-subjects-ANOVA:
    - mean value would be more common (even suggested aggregation function in documentation of AnovaRM: https://www.statsmodels.org/stable/generated/statsmodels.stats.anova.AnovaRM.html)
    - s. box plots: mean value is sometimes even outside the whisker-range (i.e. outside [Q2 - 1.5 IQR, Q3 + 1.5 * IQR])
    - median more robust considering highly diffuse data ("outliers" included)
